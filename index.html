About ICISST 2026

Innovating for a Greener, Smarter Future

The International Conference on Intelligent Systems and Sustainable Technologies aims to bring together leading academic scientists, researchers, and research scholars to exchange and share their experiences and research results.

Organized by Vidya Academy of Science and Technology, this event serves as a premier interdisciplinary platform for practitioners and educators to present and discuss the most recent innovations, trends, and concerns in the fields of AI and Engineering.
