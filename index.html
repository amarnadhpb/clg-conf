Innovating for a Greener, Smarter Future

Organized by Vidya Academy of Science and Technology, this event serves as a premier interdisciplinary platform for practitioners and educators to present and discuss the most recent innovations, trends, and concerns in the fields of AI and Engineering.

Host City

Thrissur – Cultural Capital of Kerala

Thrissur, widely celebrated as the Cultural Capital of Kerala, is a city where tradition, art, and history blend seamlessly with modern life. Famous for its classical art forms, spiritual landmarks, and grand celebrations, Thrissur offers visitors a deep and authentic cultural experience.

Notable attractions in and around Thrissur include:

  • Vadakkumnathan Temple:A UNESCO-recognized heritage site dedicated to Lord Shiva, admired for its ancient murals and traditional Kerala temple architecture.
  • Thrissur Zoo and State Museum: Exhibits regional wildlife, history, and culture.
  • Punnathur Kotta Elephant Sanctuary: A unique elephant sanctuary offering close‑up views of trained elephants.
  • Shakthan Thampuran Palace: A restored palace museum highlighting Thrissur’s royal legacy.
  • Athirappilly Waterfalls: Often called the “Niagara of India,” located a short drive from the city.

With its welcoming atmosphere and diverse attractions, Thrissur provides an enriching cultural backdrop for conference participants.
