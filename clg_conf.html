About the Conference

Bridging Academia and Industry for a Smarter Tomorrow

Our mission is to foster collaboration between world-class researchers and industry leaders in the field of Artificial Intelligence. This three-day event features deep-dive keynote speeches, technical workshops, and networking sessions designed to push the boundaries of modern technology.
